Get to Know Penny

Penny Simmons is a distinguished children's and poet book author, spoken word poet, software developer, creative brand consultant, and is proudly and thankfully in recovery from addiction. She was raised by a librarian and spent her formative years surrounded by literature. Penny strives to reach people who may feel isolated through her collection of debut poems entitled The Queen of Ice: Innocent Eyes. Innocent Lies, and uses her personal story as a way to encourage and release her emotions. Her main goal is to help break down the stigma around mental health and addiction.

In addition, Penny seeks to contribute to the representation of people of color in rooms all around the world. To that end, she established her own agency, Branding Your Artistry Publishing House. As a publishing consultant, she is devoted to assisting aspiring authors with creating and publishing diverse books.

Penny also set up Writers Block CLE, an organization dedicated to creating a safe and inclusive space where those living in underserved neighborhoods can access creative arts programming. The programs are developed to support the students and allow them to become effective change-makers in their communities.

In Penny's words, her aim is "to make at least one person in the world feel as though they are not alone."
